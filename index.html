 King Richard Rosenbaum 

"Great music. Sexy bass player. What more could you ask for? " - Albert Einstein

Hailing from the vibrant and eclectic city of Las Vegas, King Richard Rosenbaum, commonly known as KRR, is a fresh and dynamic jazz trio that has been making waves in the music scene since their formation in 2023. The band comprises three exceptionally talented musicians: Matt King on guitar, Richard Laub on bass, and Trenton Rosenbaum on drums.
KRR's journey began with a shared passion for jazz and a desire to push the boundaries of the genre. Their debut album, "Is This Jazz?", released in April 2024, is a testament to their innovative approach and musical prowess. The album blends traditional jazz elements with contemporary influences, creating a unique and captivating sound that resonates with both jazz aficionados and new listeners alike.
With each member bringing their own distinct style and expertise to the table, KRR has quickly established a reputation for their electrifying performances and intricate compositions. Matt King's guitar work is both soulful and technically impressive, Richard Laub's bass lines provide a solid and groovy foundation, and Trenton Rosenbaum's drumming adds a dynamic and rhythmic flair that ties everything together.
As KRR continues to grow and evolve, they remain committed to exploring new musical horizons and sharing their love for jazz with audiences everywhere. Keep an eye on this talented trio as they continue to redefine what it means to be a jazz band in the modern era.
